Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account through the app or web interface. Contact your organization administrator for access.

How do I upload documents?

Use the camera feature in the app to scan documents, or select from your photo library. Documents are automatically processed with OCR.

How do I assign experts?

In case details, click "Add Expert" and select from your organization's expert list. Experts will receive notifications of their assignment.

Who can I contact for technical issues?

Email stefantodoroski2011@gmail.com with a description of the issue and we'll assist you as soon as possible.
